About UmeedConnect

UmeedConnect is our 24/7 toll-free helpline that provides immediate assistance, guidance, and support to those in need. Our trained counselors and support staff are available round the clock to help individuals access resources, find solutions, and connect with the right services.

How It Works

1. Call

Dial our toll-free number anytime

2. Connect

Speak with our trained counselors

3. Support

Get the help you need

Services Offered

  • Emergency assistance and crisis intervention
  • Job search and career guidance
  • Healthcare access and referrals
  • Legal aid and counseling
  • Skill development program information
  • Mental health support
