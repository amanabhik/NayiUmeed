About ZindagiAlert

ZindagiAlert is our flagship feature designed to provide immediate assistance to those in need on the streets. Through a simple reporting system, concerned citizens can alert our network about individuals requiring urgent help, medical attention, or basic necessities.

How It Works

1. Report

Use our app or toll-free number to report someone in need

2. Respond

Our team reaches the location within 30 minutes

3. Support

Provide immediate assistance and long-term solutions

Key Features

  • Real-time location tracking for quick response
  • Priority-based alert system
  • Integration with local healthcare providers
  • Anonymous reporting option
  • Follow-up tracking system
