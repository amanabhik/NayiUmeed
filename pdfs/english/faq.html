Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is NayiUmeed?
NayiUmeed is a skill development platform dedicated to empowering individuals through education and vocational training.
Who can join NayiUmeed?
Anyone above 18 years of age who wants to learn new skills and improve their career prospects can join our platform.
Is there any registration fee?
No, all our basic courses are completely free. Some advanced certification programs may have a nominal fee.

Course Related

How do I enroll in a course?
Simply register on our platform, browse through available courses, and click on the 'Enroll' button for your chosen course.
What is the duration of courses?
Course durations vary from 4 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity and level of the course.
Will I get a certificate after completion?
Yes, you will receive a certificate upon successful completion of the course and passing the assessment.

Technical Support

What if I face technical issues?
You can contact our support team at support@nayiumeed.org or call our helpline at 1800-123-4567.
What are the system requirements?
You need a device with internet access and a modern web browser. Some courses may require additional software.

Placement Support

Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that helps connect trained candidates with potential employers.
What kind of jobs can I get after training?
Job opportunities vary based on the course you complete. Our career counselors can help you understand the prospects for each course.
